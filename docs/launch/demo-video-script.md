# Demo Video Script (45–60 seconds)

## Hook (0–8s)

What if you could generate a branded YouTube Short, TikTok, or Reel from a simple form — without paying for cloud rendering?

## Problem (8–18s)

Creating short-form videos manually is slow. You need a script, a voiceover, captions, background footage, music, and editing. Most tools are either expensive or force you into the cloud.

## What Abud Shorts Engine does (18–38s)

Abud Shorts Engine is a local-first, open-source Docker app. Choose a business template like Product Ad or Restaurant Offer, fill in the details, and the engine handles the rest:

- AI script and narration
- Automatic captions
- Background footage from Pexels
- Branded watermark, music, and outro
- A downloadable MP4

Everything runs on your own machine.

## n8n automation mention (38–48s)

It also ships with an official n8n workflow. Connect your own credentials and automate the whole pipeline, with optional YouTube upload that stays private by default.
